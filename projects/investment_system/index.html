Investment Management System

This web application is made by keeping in mind the needs of Mutual Fund Distributors as they are the primary consumers of the application.

The distributors can keep a track of the funds allocated to their clients. They can visualize investors assets through dashboard.

MF Distributor can perform CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations on their Investors and their Funds Allocated. They get the Live NAV (Net Asset Value) directly from the AMFI India Website

The Mutual Fund Distributors can download Realized Gain of Investor in a specific financial year and the investors Current Portfolio Valuation report.They can download these reports in PDF format.

Technologies Used - Python Django Chart.js SQLite3
