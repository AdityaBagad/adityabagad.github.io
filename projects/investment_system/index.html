Investment Management System

This web application is made for Mutual Fund Distributors. They get the live NAV of the mutual funds from the Official AMFI website.

The distributors can keep a track of the funds allocated to their clients. They can also download reports of particular clients in the form of pdf.

Technologies - Python Django Chart.js SQLite3
